# Your account

Manage your login credentials and personal settings.

## Change your password

1. Click your name in the nav → **Settings** (or go to `/settings`)
2. Enter your **current password**
3. Enter and confirm your **new password**
4. Save

**Password policy:**

- Minimum 8 characters
- No complexity rules (length is enough for a trusted household)

## Forgot your password?

There is no self-service "forgot password" flow. Ask your household **admin** to reset your password from the [user management](../admin/users.md) page.

## Display name

Your display name is set by the admin when your account is created. It's used for:

- @-mention autocomplete
- Activity attribution ("Alex added a restaurant")

If you need your display name changed, ask an admin.

## Roles

| Role | Your capabilities |
| ---- | ----------------- |
| **Member** | Read/write all household data, change own password |
| **Admin** | Everything above, plus user management at `/admin/users` |

## Logging out

Use **Logout** from the user menu in the nav. Your session cookie is cleared.

## Sessions

Sessions last up to 30 days. If an admin disables your account or resets your password, all your sessions are invalidated and you'll need to log in again (with the new password, if reset).
